We are Specialized in IAM Services

We help you capture the modern IAM roadmap, in an effective and sustainable manner.

01.

IAM Design

IAM is tremendous to enterprise security and the resources protected. The challenging part is that it includes a multitude of subdisciplines - Identity Management, Access Management, Identity Governance, Privileged Access Management, Crypto Management and so forth. Each enterprise will be unique and hence asking different strategies and designs to fulfill its security goal.

02

IAM Implementation

An IAM implementation should not be a simple project with a limited timeframe; instead, it requires a formal long-term program in several phases and proper governance, which are aligned to business goals. Research has shown that organizations without a well-planned program spend generally 40% more on an IAM implementation than those that do have it.

03.

IAM Support

A lot of enterprise decision makers can come to the erroneous conclusion that long-term support for IAM system is unnecessary. Although modern IAM solutions are becoming more autonomous, active involvement in IAM maintenance is still needed, especially on the aspect of utilizing the data generated by the solution tools. Staying updated is also important to the entire IAM solution due to the fact that information technologies are evolving rapidly nowadays.

04.

IAM Migration

We are in a time where a new generation of IAM solutions starting to replace old legacy IAM systems. Yet, this migration job is often tougher than expected.
